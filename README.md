## What This Repository Contains

An open-source decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) governance 
framework designed specifically for indigenous and tribal communities 
to manage shared resources, infrastructure, and territorial sovereignty 
without dependence on any external authority — government, corporation, 
or otherwise.

## Core Principles

- **Anti-extraction by design** — no single entity can vote to sell 
  community land or resources without unanimous tribal council approval
- **Constitutionally encoded exit rights** — every community member 
  retains the right to withdraw their share of community assets
- **Transparent treasury** — all community funds visible on-chain 
  to all members at all times
- **Multi-sig protection** — major decisions require approval from 
  multiple elected council members simultaneously
- **Open-source AI management** — energy, water, and life support systems 
  managed by open-source AI with full community override at any time
- **Immutable history** — all governance decisions recorded permanently 
  on-chain. Nothing can be erased or rewritten.

## Governance Structure

### Three Tiers of Authority

**Tier 1 — Individual Members**
Every adult community member holds one non-transferable governance token.
One person. One vote. Always.

**Tier 2 — Tribal Council**
Elected council of 7–12 members manages day-to-day operations.
All council actions are on-chain and publicly visible.
Council members serve fixed terms with community recall rights.

**Tier 3 — Constitutional Layer**
Certain decisions — sale of land, mineral rights, exit from the DAO —
require a supermajority (75%+) of ALL members, not just council.
These protections cannot be amended by the council alone.

## Relationship to Liberty Coin Alliance

This governance framework is the constitutional foundation of the 
Liberty Coin Alliance (₡LC) — a mineral-backed cryptocurrency 
connecting indigenous communities globally through tokenized 
natural resource sovereignty.

Every community that joins the Liberty Coin Alliance adopts this 
governance framework as their constitutional foundation.
